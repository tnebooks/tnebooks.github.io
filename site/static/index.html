TNEBooks
TNEBooks is on a mission to provide high-quality study materials to Tamil Nadu government school learners, irrespective of their socio-economic backgrounds.

Our dedicated community of volunteers passionately believes in the right to education for all. Through collaborative efforts, we create study materials aligned with the Tamil Nadu curriculum that are:

Accurate

Carefully reviewed to ensure they are error-free and academically reliable.

Simplified

Presented in a clear and easy-to-understand manner for effective learning.

Enriched

Enhanced with additional explanations, examples, illustrations, and learning aids.

Our aim is to empower every child by ensuring equal access to accurate, simplified, and enriched educational resources.
